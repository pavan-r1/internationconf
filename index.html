About the International Conference

The International Conference on Recent Advancements in Technology and Engineering - RATE 2025 is a premier event hosted by the Departments of CSE/DS/CSE(IOT)

The conference theme, Innovation & Research for a Sustainable Future, The 1st International Conference on Recent Advancements in Technology and Engineering (RATE-2025) is a distinguished annual event dedicated to showcasing technological advancements and innovations within the fields of computer science and related disciplines. This conference is scheduled to take place on November 21, 2025, at T John Institute of Technology (TJIT) in Bangalore, India. RATE-2025 serves as a platform for students, researchers, innovators, technologists, and industry professionals who are at the forefront of computer science and its allied branches, including Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Cyber Security, Data Science and cloud computing, and software engineering. The conference is supported by a robust advisory committee comprising esteemed researchers. The theme of the conference, "Innovation & Research for a Sustainable Future," encourages the exploration of cutting-edge research in core engineering and technology disciplines. We extend an invitation to academics, researchers, and industry experts to present their latest findings and engage in transformative discussions in areas such as Data Science, IoT, and Computer Science.

ABOUT THE INSTITUTE
T. John Institute of Technology (TJIT) is a private Engineering College located in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. It was established in 2006 by Dr. Thomas P. John and is part of T. John Group of Institutions. The Institute offers Undergraduate & Postgraduate programmes, and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

About the Venue:
The conference will be held at the scenic and modern campus of T. John Institute of Technology, located on Bannerghatta Road in Bangalore. The institute offers state-of-the-art conference halls, ample parking, and is easily accessible from major parts of the city.

How to Reach:
The campus is well connected by road and public transport. It is approximately 20 km from Bangalore City Railway Station and 50 km from Kempegowda International Airport.

Ms Papitha Cader

Scheduling Analyst, The University of Sydney, Australia

Ms Papitha Cader is a professional with over two decades of experience across technology, healthcare, and higher education operations. She was among the first women graduates in Computer Science Engineering in the late 1990s and has built a career that spans the Middle East, Southeast Asia, New Zealand, and Australia.

Her professional background includes supply chain optimization at Hewlett-Packard, where she solved real-world operational challenges, followed by a decade in the disability health sector, working on complex pay equity and workforce sustainability issues with the Ministry of Health. At the University of Sydney, she is recognized for streamlining processes, strengthening systems, and supporting decision-making with data-driven insights.

Academically, she holds a Master of Engineering in Computer Science from the University of Waikato, New Zealand (External Research Scholarship recipient), and is currently pursuing a Master of Public Health (MPH) at the University of Sydney, supported by a Commonwealth Supported Place (CSP) credit. Her studies focus on communicable disease control, particularly tuberculosis (TB), which she frames as a complex social disease requiring integrated biomedical, behavioral, and systemic responses.

Papitha applies systems thinking and strong analytical skills to complex problems, bridging technical expertise with practical outcomes. She blends precision with empathy, ensuring decisions are both evidence-based and grounded in real-world experiences.
